Our Story

Built on doing the job right

AG Doors started with a simple idea: garage door repairs shouldn't be confusing, overpriced, or take a week to sort out. We're a local team who turn up, diagnose the actual problem, and fix it properly the first time — backed by a warranty so you're never left wondering if it'll hold up.

Today, we've helped hundreds of satisfied customers get their garage doors working the way they should — from a quick spring replacement to a full motor upgrade. Whatever's going on with your door, chances are we've fixed one just like it before.

We're proudly local — not a call centre routing you to whoever's available, but a team who knows the area and shows up when we say we will.
