Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information when you get in touch with us.

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

AG Doors ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This page explains what personal information we collect through this website, why we collect it, and how it's handled. We keep this simple and only collect what we actually need to respond to your enquiry and provide our services.

What We Collect

When you use the enquiry form on our Contact page, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your suburb
  • Any message or details you provide about your garage door issue

We don't collect payment details, government ID numbers, or any sensitive personal information through this website.

How We Use It

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your enquiry, provide quotes, and arrange service. We don't use it for unrelated marketing, and we don't sell or rent your information to any third party.

How the Contact Form Works

Our contact form is powered by a third-party form processing service called FormSubmit, which delivers your submission directly to our business email. We don't operate our own server or database to store form submissions — FormSubmit handles the delivery, and your enquiry then lives in our email inbox like any other message we receive.

Other Services Used on This Site

This website uses a couple of external services that may collect limited technical data as part of how they operate:

  • Google Fonts — used to display the fonts on this site, which may involve your browser connecting to Google's servers.
  • Google Maps — embedded on our Service Area page to show our coverage area, subject to Google's own privacy policy.

We don't control how these third parties handle data beyond what's needed to display their service on our site — you can review Google's privacy policy directly on their website for more detail.

Your Rights

You can ask us at any time what information we hold about you, request a correction, or ask us to delete it. Just contact us using the details below and we'll action your request as quickly as we can.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this page from time to time as our website or business practices change. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, contact us at garagedoorsag@gmail.com or call 0411 419 533.
